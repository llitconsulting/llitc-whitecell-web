Our Mission

White Cell, Inc. is a non-profit organization formed to assist with a support group for patients in Northwest Arkansas. Our support group holds monthly meetings and provides resources, compassionate giving, educational lectures, and materials for patients and families living with primary immune deficiencies, hereditary angioedema, and severe allergic reactions.

What We Provide

Resources

Connecting patients and families with the information and tools they need.

Monthly Meetings

Regular support group gatherings for patients and their families.

Compassionate Giving

Providing compassionate support and assistance to those in need.

Education

Educational lectures and materials for patients and caregivers.

Who We Serve

Serving the Northwest Arkansas community of 450,000 — patients and families from newborns to 89 years old.

Primary Immune Deficiencies

Supporting those diagnosed with conditions affecting the immune system's ability to fight infection.

Hereditary Angioedema

Providing community, resources, and advocacy for patients managing hereditary angioedema.

Severe Allergic Reactions

Assisting patients and families living with severe allergic conditions and anaphylaxis.

About Us

White Cell, Inc. was founded in 2009 and received its 501(c)(3) non-profit designation in May 2010. We are committed to improving the lives of patients and families affected by immune system disorders throughout the Northwest Arkansas region.
