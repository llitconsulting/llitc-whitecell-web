Mission Statement

White Cell, Inc is a non-profit organization formed to assist with a support group for patients in
Northwest Arkansas. White Cell Support Group has monthly meetings. The support group
provides resources, compassionate giving, educational lectures and materials for patients and
families with primary immune deficiencies, hereditary angioedema and severe allergic
reactions.
Our target population is Northwest Arkansas, which includes 450,000 people. The age of our
members ranges from newborn to 89 years old.
The organization formed in 2009 and the 501c(3) letter was received in May 2010, EIN:
27-0461424.
